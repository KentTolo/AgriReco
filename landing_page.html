Empowering Lesotho's Farmers

AgroAI is a final year computer science project at the National University of Lesotho, designed to revolutionise farming practices across the country. We combine Artificial Intelligence(AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) technology to help local farmers maximize their yields and improve their livelihoods.

Our Mission

To empower Lesotho's agricultural community with smart farming solutions that increase productivity and sustainability.

Our Impact

Working with local farmers to implement AI-driven solutions that address Lesotho's unique agricultural challenges.
